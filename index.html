Welcome to mexiasian cuisine

The story

In my home country, Mexico, food plays an essential role in the life our people, so much that when you get home the first thing you will be asked will often be "¿Ya comiste?" which literally means, "Did you eat?", but conveys a deeper meaning of concern and care... or maybe it is just something we learnt from our parents.

As in Mexico, food plays an important role in many Asian countries. You might have heard "밥 먹었어 (Bap meogeosseo)?" for example, if you are into K-drama. This phrase also translates as "did you eat?", but it is often used between friends and family as greeting.
