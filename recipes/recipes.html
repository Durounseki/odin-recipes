Recipes

  • Bibimbap: Rice with mixed veggies and meat, served on a sizzling stone bowl with crispy sesame oil-fried rice and a selection of Banchan 반찬 (Banchan) -this is the ultimate Korean comfort food.
  • Bulgogi Sandwich: Inspired by the famous Bibimbap, a delicious sweet soy sauce beef sandwich.
  • Mayak Gyeran: Sweet, sour and spicy marinated eggs. Their incomparable flavor is has earned them the nickname drug eggs.
